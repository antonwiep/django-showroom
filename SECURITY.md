# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please do not open public issues for potential vulnerabilities.

Report privately to: `security@your-project-domain.example`

Include:

- Affected versions/commit
- Reproduction steps
- Impact assessment
- Suggested mitigation (if available)

## Response targets

- Initial response: within 3 business days
- Triage decision: within 7 business days
- Patch timeline: depends on severity and complexity

## Disclosure

We follow coordinated disclosure. Public advisories are published after fixes are available.
