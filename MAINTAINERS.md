## Current maintainers

## Maintainer responsibilities

- Triaging issues and PRs.
- Reviewing and merging contributions.
- Managing releases and changelog quality.
- Maintaining contributor experience and project direction.

## Becoming a maintainer

Regular contributors may be invited based on:

- History of high-quality contributions.
- Reliable and respectful collaboration.
- Willingness to support review and triage load.
